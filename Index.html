the happy florist

My love affair with flowers began over twenty years ago when I grew and sold cut stems and bouquets at the local farmers markets. My fascination with flowers took root as I cultivated and vended fresh stems and bouquets at nearby farmers' markets. Presently, our distinctive "garden style" arrangements echo this ardor for every seasonal, distinctive, or exquisite bloom.

I eschew carnations and mums, drawing inspiration instead from the kaleidoscope of hues, textures, and species sourced from global growers, big and small. Each arrangement receives meticulous packaging and is dispatched in our hallmark gift box, adorned with ribbon, tissue, and a personalized handwritten card from you.
